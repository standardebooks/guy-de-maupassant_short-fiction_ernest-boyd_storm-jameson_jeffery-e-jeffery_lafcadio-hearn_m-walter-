Colophon

Short Fiction
was compiled from short stories published between 1876 and 1921 by
Guy de Maupassant.
They were translated from French between 1901 and 1949 by
Ernest Boyd, Storm Jameson, Jeffery E. Jeffery, Lafcadio Hearn, M. Walter Dunne, Henry C. Olinger, Albert M. Cohn-McMaster, Dora Knowlton Ranous, Bigelow, Brown & Co., Inc., and Francis Steegmuller.

This ebook was produced for
Standard Ebooks
by
Vince Rice,
and is based on digital scans from
various sources.

The cover page is adapted from
Château Noir,
a painting completed between 1900 and 1904 by
Paul Cézanne.

League Spartan and Sorts Mill Goudy
typefaces created in 2014 and 2009 by
The League of Moveable Type.
